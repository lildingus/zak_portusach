About Me

My name is Zakary Portusach, I live in Campbell, CA, and I attend San Jose State University in pursuit of a BFA degree in the Digital Media Arts field.
Due to my upbringing being surrounded by skateboarding and other counter cultures, I have developed a style to let me do whatever I want, where I want, and how I want it; and I believe Digital Media Art is a catalyst for achieving this personal mantra.
